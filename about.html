About Me

I'm Alagbe Fareed Adebayo, a Frontend Developer based in Lagos, Nigeria, with a passion for technology and web development. I specialize in building modern, responsive, and user-friendly web applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My journey into tech began with a curiosity about how websites work, which gradually developed into a strong interest in creating engaging digital experiences. Through continuous practice and learning, I have developed the skills needed to build clean, functional, and responsive user interfaces.

I am committed to continuous learning, exploring emerging technologies, and collaborating on projects that solve real-world problems and create meaningful impact.
